NETDAN

Demonstrates the NETDAN chatbot

The assistive tool NETDAN is a chat and voice bot that detects and informs the users of the status of internet connectivity of the “common” sites.


This page shows how the NETDAN internet site status checker Bot and messenger would look like on a website. Go ahead and interact with it by clicking the blue messenger in the botton right corner of the page or just text Hi in the web bot already launched on the right of this page.

Alternatively, interact with the bot via phone by dialing +256 800 113 269
